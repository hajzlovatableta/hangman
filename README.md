# Hangman

## How to play
- Press letter keys **Aâ€“Z** on your keyboard to guess the hidden word.
- Correct letters will appear in the word.
- Wrong letters increase your mistakes.
- You lose when you reach the maximum number of mistakes.
- You win when all letters in the word are revealed.

## Score / Best score
- Your **score** is the number of unique letter guesses you used to win.
- Lower score = better.
